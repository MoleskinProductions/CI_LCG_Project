# Interface Variables

## Global vocabulary (A0 curated)
- `primitive budget / silent premise`: count and type of assumptions that become implicit when a chart is rewritten.
- `ledger stability / taxonomy drift`: whether seam categories remain stable over iterative updates.
- `observability equivalence class`: which observables remain equivalent under allowed representation/gauge transforms.
- `dimensionless drift`: residual change in ratio-level quantities after rescaling/re-description.
- `orientation residue`: closure-stable residue tied to orientation/polarity after transport.
- `constraint-order coupling`: whether ordering constraints are intrinsic to structure or externally imposed.
- `local arrow persistence`: survival of local directional structure under global re-indexing.
- `loop/closure residue`: non-local residue detectable only through closed-loop comparison.
